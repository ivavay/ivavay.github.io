About

Hey — Thanks for dropping by!

My name is Ivy Chen, a third-year studying Cognitive Science at UC Santa Cruz.

Design to me holds the power to catalyze social and environmental change. As an aspiring product designer, I want to learn more about the people who are struggling at the margins of society and try my best to understand the multifaceted issues that these communities face. I believe that design needs to listen more to the underserved and equip them with tools to empower their lives.

At school, I spend most of my time designing interfaces and conducting HCI research at the Tech4good lab. Additionally, I co-lead design at Cruzhacks 2020. In my free time, I like to explore on my skateboard, write poetry, and shoot photography. This site was inspired by Chinese calligraphy.

Let’s chat more over coffee?
